🍜 Tonkotsu Ramen

Ingredients

  • 1 liter pork broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small piece ginger (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 200g ramen noodles
  • 1 boiled egg
  • 100g chashu pork
  • 2 tbsp green onions (sliced)
  • 1 sheet nori (seaweed)

Steps

  1. Sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add pork broth and milk, then simmer for 20–30 minutes.
  3. Stir in soy sauce and mix well.
  4. Cook 200g ramen noodles separately.
  5. Place noodles in a bowl and pour in the hot tonkotsu broth.
  6. Top with chashu pork, boiled egg, green onions, and nori.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy.

🍜 Shoyu Ramen

Ingredients

  • 1 liter chicken broth
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce (shoyu)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small piece ginger (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 200g ramen noodles
  • 1 boiled egg
  • 100g chicken or pork slices
  • 2 tbsp green onions (sliced)
  • 1 sheet nori (seaweed)

Steps

  1. Sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add 1 liter chicken broth and simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Add soy sauce, mirin, and sesame oil. Mix well.
  4. Cook 200g ramen noodles separately.
  5. Put noodles in a bowl and pour the hot broth.
  6. Add egg, chicken or pork, green onions, and nori on top.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy.
